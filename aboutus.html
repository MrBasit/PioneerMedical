About Us

Our Mission:

Our mission is to improve access to health care, by pairing patients that need telehealth care, with providers who would like to see patients via telehealth, and to increase organizational efficiencies through telehealth technologies.

Our Vision:

Is to fully incorporate telehealth technologies into the routine business practices of our clients and providers.  This will allow for better access to care, more efficiency, and lower the costs of care.

Our Team:

Our team is made up Telehealth Industry experts, with a combined experience of over 30 years in the healthcare staffing industry. We have staffed thousands of medical professionals across several specialties.   The team made the decision to innovate within the Telehealth industry, and Pioneer Telemedicine was born.

What Do We Do?

The team at Pioneer Telemedicine connects providers with clinics, hospitals, nursing homes and other organizations. We have a large network of clinics, and providers and consult through the process of seeing patients online.  If you are a provider or administrator that is interested in learning more about Pioneer Telemedicine and how you can benefit from working with us send us a info@pioneertelemedicine.com.
